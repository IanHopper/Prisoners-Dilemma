Prisoner's Dilemma

You and an accomplice have been arrested and the cops are interrogating you separately.
Each of you can choose whether to stonewall the cops (COOPERATE) or rat out the other guy (DEFECT).

If either of you defects and the other cooperates, the defector gets 5pts and the cooperator 1pt.
If you both defect, you each get 1 point.
If you both cooperate, you each get 3 points.
